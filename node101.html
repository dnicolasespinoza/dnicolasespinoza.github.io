Tensile fractures and wellbore breakdown

Wellbore tensile (or open mode) fractures occur when the minimum principal stress on the wellbore wall goes below the limit for tensile stress: the tensile strength. Unconsolidated sands have no tensile strength. Hence, an open-mode fracture occurs as soon as effective stress goes to zero.
