1.3 Problems

Completion through horizontal wellbores and multistage hydraulic fracturing has dramatically impacted the production of oil and gas in the US.

  1. Read the summaries of the International Energy Agency 2018 reports: https://www.iea.org/oil2018/ and https://www.iea.org/gas2018/.
  2. What is the share of shale gas with respect to total gas production in the US for 2017? Out of shale gas, how much does it come from the Permian Basin and the Appalachian Basin in the same year?
  3. What is the share of light tight oil (LTO) with respect to total oil production in the US for 2018? How many times did LTO production increase with respect to 2010?
